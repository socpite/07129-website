Computer Vision

2025 Computer Vision

Name three applications of Computer Vision you can recognize on your phone

  • Face recognition for unlocking your device
  • Augmented reality filters (e.g., Snapchat/Instagram lenses)
  • Text scanning (OCR) in your camera app

Take a photo of your desk and ask ChatGPT to identify or name all the objects

You can upload the image and prompt ChatGPT to list and name each visible object on your desk.

How many steps are involved in this identification process?

The process typically involves three steps: image upload, object detection, and object labeling.

Can you identify any challenges for ChatGPT

Challenges include overlapping objects, poor lighting, and ambiguous object boundaries affecting detection accuracy.

Ask ChatGPT to highlight or circle a specific item on your desk. Describe the results and possible challenges

ChatGPT may return coordinates or a bounding box description but may struggle with precise localization or visual markup without proper visualization support.

Several applications involve decision-making processes that rely on Computer Vision.

Examples include autonomous vehicle navigation, identity verification, and quality inspection in manufacturing.

Name some applications that you believe are not trustworthy or safe.

  • Unregulated facial surveillance systems
  • Deepfake detection tools with high false positives
  • Emotion recognition software claiming to read feelings
