Artificial Intelligence

2025 AI

How would you define Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence is the field of study and engineering focused on creating machines and software that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, such as perception, reasoning, learning, and decision-making.

AI has existed for nearly 70 years. Why is it experiencing an impressive growth right now?

Recent growth is driven by advances in computational power (GPUs and specialized accelerators), access to large datasets, improved algorithms (deep learning), and investment in AI infrastructure and research.

Can you identify three domains or classes of problems where current AI models still face significant challenges?

  • Common-sense reasoning and understanding unstructured context
  • Causal inference and robust decision-making under uncertainty
  • Generalization to out-of-distribution scenarios and adversarial robustness

Can you name at least three application sectors where robots are widely used, and explain why they are employed in those sectors?

  • Manufacturing: for precision assembly, welding, and high-volume production
  • Healthcare: for surgical assistance, rehabilitation, and sterilized tasks
  • Logistics and warehousing: for automated sorting, packing, and inventory management

Do you believe that the convergence of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and fully autonomous robotics is imminent and could potentially have a major impact on the world?

While progress in AI and robotics is rapid, true AGI and fully autonomous robotics convergence remains uncertain; widespread impact is likely gradual as technical, ethical, and regulatory challenges are addressed.
